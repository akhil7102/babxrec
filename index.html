🔒 Privacy Policy for BabxRec

Effective Date: July 28, 2025

Last Updated: July 28, 2025

BabxRec is developed by BabxStudios. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use BabxRec and our related services.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

BabxRec is an offline screen recording software. We do not collect any of the following:

  • Personal information (name, email, address)
  • Screen content or audio
  • System information or usage statistics
  • Location data or IP addresses

Your recordings are saved locally on your device and are not uploaded or shared by the app.

2. No Internet Tracking

BabxRec does not require an internet connection for its core functions. It does not use analytics tools, cookies, or third-party trackers.

3. Optional Updates

When checking for updates (optional), BabxRec may connect to our secure server to verify the latest version. No personal data is transmitted during this process.

4. Third-Party Services

If you choose to use features like cloud storage integrations (Pro or Enterprise plans), those features may follow their own privacy policies. We encourage you to read the policies of those third-party services separately.

5. Your Control

You remain in full control of your recordings. BabxRec does not transmit, back up, or access any user files. You may delete or manage recordings on your device at any time.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you may contact us at:
