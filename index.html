How BabxRec Works

Recording your screen has never been easier. Follow these simple steps to get started.

1

Download & Install

Download the EXE file from Google Drive. The installer will be added to your system. Run it as administrator to launch the app.

2

Select Recording Area

Choose what you want to record - a particular screen or the entire screen. Click "Select Source" to confirm your choice.

3

Enable Audio

Turn on the audio recording option to capture system sounds and microphone input along with your screen recording.

4

Start Recording

Click the start button to begin recording. Capture your content with high quality and smooth performance.

5

Process Recording

After clicking stop, open the recording and drag the timeline to the end so it processes properly in the app.

6

Save Your Recording

Click the 3 dots, select "Download", and choose the directory where you want to save your recorded file.
